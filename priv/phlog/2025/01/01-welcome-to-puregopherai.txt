Welcome to PureGopherAI Phlog!

This is the official phlog (Gopher blog) for the PureGopherAI server.

PureGopherAI is a pure Elixir Gopher server with native AI inference
powered by Bumblebee. It runs locally on your machine, supporting
both clearnet and Tor hidden service connections.

Key Features:
- RFC 1436 compliant Gopher protocol
- Native AI inference with GPT-2 (no external APIs)
- Apple Silicon Metal GPU acceleration
- Conversation memory for chat sessions
- Multiple AI models and personas
- Rate limiting and response caching
- Tor hidden service support

Stay tuned for more updates!
